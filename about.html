About Us



We, Electro-Med, commenced our operations in 1977 as manufacturer of a wide range of Digital Instruments and LCD Display Board. Our range of products is inclusive of LED Display, LED Display Board and Line Frequency Monitors. Owing to our offerings of unsurpassable quality, we have been able to establish a commendable reputation in the industry. These offerings are patronized by a large number of customers, pertaining to several industries. We design these display systems keeping in mind the exact requirements of our customers.

We have established a state-of-the-art infrastructure that is spread over a vast area. Equipped with high-tech machinery and multiple amenities, our infrastructure is instrumental in carrying out our functions in a hassle free manner. We have segregated our entire facility into multiple divisions to ensure that maximum operational efficiency is achieved. These units are production department, quality assurance department, designing unit, administration and logistics unit. These units are efficiently managed by our team of dexterous professionals. Working in close coordination with each other, these units achieve spectacular results consistently. Our team members are proficient in their respective areas of expertise and strive to perform to the best of their capabilities.

We are a proprietorship firm established under the visionary guidance of, Mr. Pushkar Srivastava. Through his vast domain expertise and brilliant leadership skills, he has steered the organization on the path to success. We offer fair business terms and are highly regarded in the industry.
