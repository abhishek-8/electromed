OUR HISTORY


ELECTRO-MED has a team of dedicated engineers with varied experience in the field of electronic instrumentation, displays and microcontroller based systems. Led by our proprietor, Mr. Pushkar Srivastava, B E (IIT-Roorkee), we have been able to attain the satisfaction of our clients. His in depth knowledge and experience of 42years have helped us to position ourselves as a reputed name in this industry.

1970

Continuous innovation and rapid transformation have been themes throughout Electro-Med's history, which the company traces to the 1970s with the installation of relay testing system in RDSO, Lucknow.


1990

The company built its reputation primarily as a high performance digital instruments manufacturer and supplier across in industrial sector in India. By the late 1990s, Electro-Med began offering a new breed of embedded solutions to clients—solutions that aligned organizations' technologies, and processes with their strategies.


2010

Throughout its history, Electro-Med has expanded its offerings and capitalized on evolving emerging trends and technologies to benefit its clients.
The company pioneered embedded systems; led the deployment of electronic display boards, and digital instruments; and has established itself as a leader in today's marketplace.
