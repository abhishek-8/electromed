OUR CLIENTS


We are a client friendly organization. We deliver products as per client’s specific demands. With our advanced production facility and cutting edge technology, we are able to develop innovative and user-friendly products for our clients.



POWER PLANTS

We have been supplying range of MW indicators to numerous power plants of Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Kerala, Neyveli, TANGEDCO Chennai and NTPCs units etc. We manufacture and assemble a comprehensive range of LED display boards which shows environmental / pollution control parameters at NTPC Unchahar, NTPC Badarpur, NTPC Rihand, NTPC Tanda, and NTPC Sipat. Recently we have completed two more large display boards for NTPC Unchahar.



SUGAR MILLS

Over the years we have been supplying Digital Tachometers, Temperature Indicators, Line Frequency Monitors, Weighing Scale monitors, Flow monitors, Bag counters and pH meters to prestigious mills of Balrampur group, Bajaj group, Dhampur group, Hargaon, Bannari Amman, Ponni sugars, Nirani sugars, KBD sugars to name a few. Our Weighing Scale Monitors and Flow monitors have been designed especially for installation in Sugar Industry. These products have contributed in improving the operating efficiency of the mills.






INDUSTRY

We provide a wide range of both moving or static electronic LED display and other electronic instruments for industrial application. We have been supplying an environmental parameter display board to IISCO Steel Plant of Steel Authority Of India Limited, HINDALCO Renukoot, HINDALCO, Mahan & Hi-TECH Carbon. Recently we are working on two LED display boards of HINDALCO, Renukoot and one LED display board of HINDALCO Mahan.



OTHERS

We have installed a large display system at The High Court in Patna where online case numbers are displayed at fourteen distant locations within the Court campus. We provide wide range of independent as well as master/slave Clock with or without GPS for hospitals, clinics and Air force stations etc.
