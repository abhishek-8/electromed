What we do

ELECTRO-MED is an established and leading manufacturer of electronic products in India. We manufacture and assemble a comprehensive range of moving as well as static electronic LED displays besides a wide range of electronic instruments for industrial application

Why Electro-med?

ELECTRO-MED has a team of dedicated engineers with varied experience in the field of electronic instrumentation, displays and microcontroller based systems. We have in-house R & D facility for the development of microprocessor based systems, design of PC Boards and mechanical assemblies. our in house R & D, Manufacturing and Quality Control practices ensure that the product you buy is the best in terms of technology, and consistently reliable in terms of Standards and Quality. At ELECTRO-MED, we are committed to bring to our customers, best of the innovations & quality.
